The files named KitRounded-*.ttf in this folder are SIL Open Font License 1.1
copies of the Nunito family from Google Fonts, registered in pubspec.yaml as
the family "KitRounded" so the app builds out of the box.

To use the commercial Kit Rounded typeface (Darden Studio), replace these
files with your licensed Kit Rounded weights (ExtraBold, SemiBold, Regular,
SemiBold Italic, Light) and keep the same filenames or update pubspec.yaml.

Nunito: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Nunito
